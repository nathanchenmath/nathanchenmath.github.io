ROB-AG Conference

Date

October 28-30, 2026

Location

Stony Brook University and the Simons Center for Geometry and Physics

About

Welcome to ROB-AG, a conference on recent outcomes in birational and complex algebraic geometry. This conference will mark Rob Lazarsfeld's retirement from Stony Brook University.
