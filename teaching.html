Course Description

This is a continuation of the material covered in the first semester, namely a complex analytic introduction to algebraic geometry. Among other things, we will discuss: higher dimensional manifolds, Hodge structures, polarizations, complex tori and abelian varieties, deformations of complex structures.

Prerequisites

A solid understanding of Hartshorne, Chapters II.1--II.5:

  • Chapter II.1: Sheaves
  • Chapter II.2: Schemes
  • Chapter II.3: First Properties of Schemes
  • Chapter II.4: Separated and Proper Morphisms
  • Chapter II.5: Sheaves of Modules

Textbooks

  • Algebraic geometry by Hartshorne
  • Algebraic geometry II by Mumford & Oda
